Hexcabulary

Search for vocabulary words hidden in a hexagonal game board. Similar to classic word search games, but with two additional search dimensions and novel hiding strategies such as snake and boundary-rollover. Enjoy.

This is a work in progress. Feedback/bug reports appreciated.
